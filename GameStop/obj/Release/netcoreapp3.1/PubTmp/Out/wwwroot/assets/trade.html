How To Sell Your Items

FIND PRODUCT VALUES 24/7

The GameStop website and app both have up-to-the-minute values for electronics, consoles, games, accessories, collectibles, and more! Take a look today to see what your stuff's worth.

COME IN & MAKE THE SALE

Head to your local GameStop. Bring any cords, chargers, and accessories that go with your item. Charge it up, delete any personal info, and remove any SIM/memory cards.

GET PAID YOUR WAY

You can choose to receive in-store-credit for max-value on your items, or get paid in cash. And remember, PowerUp Rewards Pro Members get 10% extra in value on most items. See trade value for details.

YOUR ITEM IS RESTORED

Every item is fully tested for functionality and repaired and restored to a like-new state. We also thoroughly clean and disinfect every item prior to resell.
